About Us

At Matrix Water Pipes and Fittings, we are dedicated to providing top-quality plumbing solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Located in the heart of Nairobi, we specialize in the supply and distribution of a wide range of products, including UPVC, PVC, PPR, and HDPE pipes and fittings. Our focus is on delivering durable, efficient, and cost-effective solutions that meet the highest industry standards.

With a strong commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, Matrix has built a reputation for excellence. We partner with leading manufacturers to ensure that our products not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients. Whether it's for water supply, drainage, or plumbing systems, we offer a complete range of solutions tailored to the specific needs of our customers.

At Matrix, we believe in creating lasting relationships with our clients by providing exceptional service, technical expertise, and a deep understanding of their unique requirements. Our team of skilled professionals is always ready to assist you in selecting the right products for your project, ensuring reliable performance and long-term value.
